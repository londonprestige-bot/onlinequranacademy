Our Noble Mission

At Al Fatima Academy (A proud project of Al Quran International), we aim to provide an easy, interactive, and highly professional environment for kids and adults alike to learn the Holy Quran from the comfort of their homes.

We understand that correct Quranic recitation with proper Tajweed rules is crucial for every Muslim. Under the umbrella of Al Quran International, we use cutting-edge online platforms like Zoom and Google Meet to offer 1-on-1 personalized attention, ensuring every student progresses at their own comfortable pace.
